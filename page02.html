Why are people worried about AI taking their jobs?

According to a report done by Goldman Sachs in March, they predicted that as many as 300 million jobs could be disrupted by the new technology and the leading advancements in AI has workers already worrying that their jobs which could soon be replaced by this technology. People working in art and design say that this future has already arrived.

What sparked this issue?

AI has caused a lot of commotion in the art and music industry. Many artists and musicians are worried about losing their jobs, AI devaluing their work and copyright infringement since AI can just replicate and manipulate artists existing work without getting permission or giving them credit.
